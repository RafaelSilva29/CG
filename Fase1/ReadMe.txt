Compile: 
	> make all

Generator: 
	> ./generator <form> <length> <height>.. <file_name>

RenderScene:
	> ./renderscene XML/<file_name>

-> Examples:

- Plane:
   > ./generator plane 3 5 plane
- Box:
   > ./generator box 3 4 5 box
- Sphere
   > ./generator sphere 5 15 15 sphere
- Cone:
   > ./generator cone 5 10 10 10 cone
